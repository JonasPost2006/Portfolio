Fuzzing

The Metis Montessori Lyceum Amsterdam, where I attend high school, collaborates with VUSEC, a cyber security department within the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, to provide a course on fuzzing. Fuzzing, in essence, is providing invalid or unexpected inputs to see how the program handles it and if it crashes, to find invulnerabilities. Upon finishing the Fuzzing course with distinction, I recieved an official certificate from VUSEC.
